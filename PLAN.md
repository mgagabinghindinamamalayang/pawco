# Pawco Pulse — One-Page GDD

## Pitch
A cute pixel cat sits at the bottom. **Drag** to move its paw and catch falling treats on the beat. The game gets faster. Miss too many → sad meow.

Inspired by *Like A Dino!*, but cat paw + treats instead of dino neck + notes.

## Core Loop
1. Cute soft song starts  
2. Treats fall from the top  
3. Drag mouse / finger → paw tip follows left–right  
4. Catch on the hit line → Perfect + combo  
5. Miss → lose a life (3 lives)  
6. Speed & spawn rate ramp up over time  
7. Game over → score, perfects, best combo, grade  

## Controls
- **Desktop:** move / drag mouse  
- **Mobile:** touch-drag  
- Catch zone = paw tip only  

## Scoring
| Event | Points |
|---|---|
| Perfect catch | 100 × combo |
| Good catch | 60 × combo |
| Yarn bonus | +50 |
| Combo | +1 per catch, resets on miss |

**Lives:** 3.

## Speed Curve
| Time | Feel |
|---|---|
| 0–15s | slow / sparse |
| 15–35s | medium |
| 35–60s | fast |
| 60s+ | frantic (soft cap) |

## Screens
1. Title → Play  
2. Play (score, combo, lives)  
3. Results (grade + stats + Again)  

## Audio
Soft cute “Like A Dino”-style procedural song: sine lead, soft chords, gentle kick/hat.
