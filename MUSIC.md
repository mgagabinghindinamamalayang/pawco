# Music + chart

- `music.mp3` — Bongo Cat Cupid cover
- `chart.json` — note times from the audio (taps + holds)

Gameplay:
- **3…2…1 countdown** before the song starts
- First notes begin ~5s into the track (intro breathe)
- **Tap note** → one fish
- **Long note** → long orange fish (hold your paw under it)

If timing feels early/late, tweak `TRACK_OFFSET` in `game.js`.
